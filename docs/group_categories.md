# 43 Meaningful Group Categories

Selected examples by domain — the full list lives in **Appendix B2** of the codebook accompanying the PSoGA database.

## Demographic
- Children
- Young people
- Elderly people
- Women
- Men
- Adults

## Economic
- Employees & workers
- Employers & business owners
- Lower class
- Upper class
- Middle class
- Farmers

## Professional
- Healthcare professionals
- Education professionals
- Civil servants
- Law enforcement
- Sociocultural professionals
- White-collar workers

## Identity / Status
- LGBTQI
- Migrants & refugees
- Ethnic & national communities
- Religious communities
- People with disabilities
- Homeless people

## Residual
- **Other** — for groups that don't fit any specified category. About 8.3% of detected group tokens fall here.

> Around **94%** of detected group tokens receive at least one category. Intersectional groups (e.g. *"young women"*) are assigned to multiple categories simultaneously via the multi-label classifier.
